Our Story
“

Where artistry meets ambition —
and softness carries strength

Velvet & Valor was born from a journey shaped by three defining places. Scotland formed the foundation — a deep respect for horses and nature, alongside an early love for art and interiors. London refined that instinct, forging discipline, leadership, and commercial clarity through years of leading, and growing within a demanding corporate landscape. Dubai, UAE became the catalyst — inspired by the region's love for horses, and incredible art & design, it offered the space, energy, and entrepreneurial freedom to finally bring vision into form.

At its core, Velvet & Valor is led by art. Each piece begins as a creative expression, designed to remind you of your love for the sport, the bond you share with your horse, and the way you choose to show up — committed, disciplined, and intentional. Inspired by the elegance and power of the horse, the brand embodies softness with purpose and strength with restraint.

More than equestrian, Velvet & Valor is a mindset. Designed for those who carry resilience, ambition, and quiet confidence wherever they go, our collections are released as considered, fashion-forward drops with meaning. These are not simply accessories, but thoughtful pieces of art — created to be worn, lived in, and returned to as markers of growth.

As the brand evolves, so too will its expressions — from equestrian accessories to journals and objects designed to support reflection, self-development, and personal progress. Whether growing as riders or as people, Velvet & Valor stands for courage, discipline, and the belief that true power lies in how you show up — with grace, intention, and heart.

Kate Luthra, Founder & Creative Director

The Craft

Four Steps.
A Thousand Hours of Mastery.

01

🏛

Material Selection

Full-grain hides are inspected by eye at a tannery outside Florence. Only the top 8% of each hide is used — the rest returned.

02

✂

Hand Cutting

Each panel is hand-cut using century-old brass templates. No laser, no die-cutting — only a steady hand and a sharp blade.

03

✦

Gold Hardware

Solid brass findings are plated to 24K gold microns — a standard we borrowed from Swiss watchmaking — and set by hand.

04

⚖

72-Point Inspection

Before packaging, every case passes a 72-point quality inspection. Fewer than 1 in 12 make it through on the first review.
