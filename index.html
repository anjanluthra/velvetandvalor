Our Story
“

Where artistry meets ambition

Velvet & Valor was born from a lifelong love of horses and art. Founded by Kate Luthra, the brand draws on her journey from the Scottish countryside to the equestrian world of Dubai — creating accessories that capture the elegance and power of the horse.

Each piece is designed as a creative expression: a reminder of the bond you share with your horse and the way you choose to show up. More than equestrian, Velvet & Valor is a mindset — for those who carry resilience, ambition, and quiet confidence wherever they go.
