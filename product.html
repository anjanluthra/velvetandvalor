The Cavalry

Named for Those
Who Ride First

Kate Luthra conceived The Cavalry after years of competing across Europe and growing frustrated by the absence of luxury equestrian accessories that could hold their own in a Mayfair dinner or an arena warm-up. Midnight burgundy ‚Äî the colour of polished dress uniforms and old port by candlelight. The 24K gold hardware references the buttons that once gleamed on parade grounds from London to Delhi.

Every detail in The Cavalry is a decision Kate made herself, from the particular tannery in Florence to the angle of the saddle stitching. It is a case for people who move through the world with intention, and who believe that what they carry should reflect who they are.
