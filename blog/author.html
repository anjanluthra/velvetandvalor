The Journal / Kate Luthra
KL

Founder & Creative Director

Kate Luthra

Kate Luthra founded Velvet & Valor in 2026 from her atelier, driven by a lifelong passion for equestrian sport and a frustration with the lack of truly elevated accessories for riders. A former competitive showjumper and graduate of the London College of Fashion, Kate oversees every element of design — from the sourcing of Italian full-grain leather to the final 72-point quality inspection.

Through The Journal, she shares her knowledge of leather craft, the equestrian world, and the philosophy behind building heirlooms rather than accessories.
