Our Story
“

Where artistry meets ambition

Velvet & Valor exists to inspire you — as an equestrian and in everyday life — to show up with strength, resilience, and passion. Born from a lifelong connection to horses, a passion for design, and a journey rooted in self-development, the brand reflects a deeper purpose that goes beyond the sport itself.

Founded by Kate Luthra, Velvet & Valor is shaped by her path from the Scottish countryside to London’s corporate world — and ultimately refined in the UAE, where her return to riding marked a pivotal new chapter. Surrounded by a culture that deeply respects the horse, she rediscovered the essence of the sport: discipline, strength, humility, and quiet confidence.

Returning to riding brought clarity — a powerful reminder of the trust and connection between horse and rider, unlike anything else. It reinforced the importance of presence, and the power of being willing to fail, learn, and grow through consistency. At the heart of the brand lies its name: Velvet & Valor — a symbol of softness and strength, of poise grounded in courage.

After years of leading, mentoring, and investing in personal growth, creating Velvet & Valor was a natural progression — an expression of both purpose and passion. Each piece is a creative expression: a reminder of the bond you share with your horse and the way you choose to show up. More than equestrian, it is a mindset for those who carry resilience, ambition, and quiet confidence wherever they go.

Kate Luthra, Founder
